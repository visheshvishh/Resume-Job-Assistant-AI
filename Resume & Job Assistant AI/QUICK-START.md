## 🔍 How to Use Effectively

### 1. Optimize Your Resume
- Use standard section headers: Skills, Experience, Education
- Include years in date ranges
- Add links: GitHub, LinkedIn, Portfolio
- Use bullet points for achievements
- Quantify: "reduced by 40%", "managed team of 5"

### 2. Paste Complete Job Description
- Include full requirements section
- Include nice-to-have skills
- Include company details
- Don't truncate or paraphrase

### 3. Review Feedback Carefully
- ❌ **Missing skills**: Add if you actually have them
- ⚠️ **Low keyword match**: Mirror exact terminology
- ⚠️ **Format issues**: Improve structure
- ✅ **Strengths**: Highlight prominently

### 4. Check Job Links
- Click "Open" to visit job portal
- Searches are pre-filtered by your skills
- Use multiple sources for maximum leads

### 5. Export & Share
- Download report for your records
- Share with career coaches
- Track improvements over time

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes! Everything runs in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

**Q: Can I upload a Word document?**
A: Yes! DOCX, PDF, and TXT formats all supported.

**Q: Why is my score low?**
A: Common reasons:
- Missing keywords from job description
- Poor resume formatting
- Lacks years of experience
- Different terminology used

**Q: Can I improve my score?**
A: Yes! Follow the feedback suggestions:
1. Add matching keywords
2. Quantify achievements
3. Use standard section headers
4. Include education/dates
5. Add GitHub/LinkedIn links

**Q: What's the perfect score?**
A: 75+ is considered excellent match. But focus on actual fit over score.

**Q: How many jobs will I find?**
A: 10 carefully selected links from multiple portals (LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, etc.)

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Yes! Download all files and open `index.html` locally. Works without internet.

**Q: Does it support other formats?**
A: Currently PDF, DOCX, TXT. You can copy-paste text from other formats too.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### File upload not working
- Check file size (max 10 MB)
- Check file format (PDF, DOCX, TXT only)
- Try paste method instead

### Can't extract PDF text
- PDF might be scanned image (not searchable text)
- Try converting to text first or paste manually
- Try different PDF or use TXT format

### Score seems inaccurate
- Ensure you pasted full job description
- Use resume with clear sections
- Include actual relevant keywords you know

### Job links not working
- Requires internet connection
- May change over time (job portals update)
- Use search bar on portal for manual search

## 📚 Understanding Your Score

### Score 80-100: ✅ Excellent Match
**What it means**: Your resume strongly aligns with the job.
**Action**: Apply with confidence!

### Score 65-79: ✅ Strong Match
**What it means**: Good fit with room for improvement.
**Action**: Add suggested keywords and apply.

### Score 50-64: ℹ️ Good Match
**What it means**: Decent overlap but gaps exist.
**Action**: Address feedback suggestions before applying.

### Score 35-49: ⚠️ Fair Match
**What it means**: Significant skills gaps.
**Action**: Build missing skills or keep looking.

### Score 0-34: ❌ Weak Match
**What it means**: Resume doesn't align well.
**Action**: Don't apply yet; rework your resume.
